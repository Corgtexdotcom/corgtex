Corgtex is an AI-native workspace operating system designed for self-managed organizations and progressive enterprises. It replaces fragmented tooling with a single intelligent environment that integrates governance, finance, and your entire AI workforce.

You can think of Corgtex as a platform built on three core pillars: **Know**, **Decide**, and **See**.

  </Card>
  <Card title="Decide" icon="gavel" href="/decide/governance-overview">

  </Card>
  <Card title="See" icon="eye" href="/see/finance-overview">

## Built for Scale and Autonomy

Modern organizations move too fast for traditional hierarchical management. Corgtex is designed to distribute autonomy while maintaining rigorous visibility and compliance. 

- **The Daily Surface:** Every morning, Corgtex provides you with a single page—a personalized daily newspaper—showing briefings, anomalies, costs, and governance decisions relevant to your specific role.
- **Methodology Agnostic:** Whether you use O2, Holacracy, Sociocracy, or a custom self-management model, Corgtex adapts to your organizational rulebook.
- **MCP Integrations:** Bring your governed Corgtex data directly into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini natively using the Model Context Protocol (MCP).
