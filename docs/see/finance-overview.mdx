The **See** pillar of Corgtex solves the black-box problem of artificial intelligence and corporate finance. 

If you are going to treat your AI agents like a workforce, you need to be able to afford them, track their ROI, and allocate their resources with the same rigor you apply to human employees.

## The Problem with SaaS Billing

When an organization buys a standard AI software suite, finance sees a single, massive "$25,000/mo API bill." It is incredibly difficult to attribute that cost to actual business impact. Did we spend that on generating cold sales emails or writing code? 

Corgtex re-architects this into an explicit, transparent financial ledger.

## Finance Capabilities

Corgtex integrates finance directly into the operating structure of your Circles:

- **Visibility:** You can see every AI agent and human team, what they are doing, and exactly what resources they are consuming in real-time.
- **Ledger Accounts:** Every Circle maintains its own internal ledger account.
- **Cycles & Allocations:** Budgets are allocated dynamically per cycle, rather than locked into rigid annual budgets. [Learn more &rarr;](/see/budgets)
- **Spend Requests:** Money isn't spent in the shadows. Expenditures (both fiat and token costs) are surfaced through consent-based approval workflows. [Learn more &rarr;](/see/spend-requests)
- **Attribution:** Costs are attributed to *real work* and specific roles, not just "the IT department."

## Tracking Your AI Workforce

By bringing the finance layer directly into the platform, Corgtex enables organizations to see exactly what their intelligence layer costs, and rapidly optimize resources where they are generating the most value.
